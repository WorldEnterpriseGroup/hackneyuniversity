About HUPP

At Hackney University, we are committed to ensuring that a world-class education is within reach. The Hackney University Pell Promise (HUPP) is our pledge to make our exceptional, boutique education even more accessible to eligible students from low-to-moderate income households.

What is HUPP?

HUPP is designed to cover the remaining tuition and mandatory fees for eligible first-time, degree-seeking undergraduate students – after all available scholarships and grants have been applied. The promise is renewable for up to eight semesters, provided students remain enrolled full-time, maintain good academic standing, and meet program requirements.

Eligibility Requirements

  • File a Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) by the priority deadline and demonstrate need.
  • Maintain a minimum High School GPA of 2.95 or above.
  • Submit a deposit confirming your intent to enroll.
  • Enroll as a traditional, full-time first-year student at Hackney University.
  • Reside in our exclusive on-campus luxury housing.
  • Pursue your first bachelor’s degree.
  • Have a household adjusted gross income (AGI) of $75,000 or less.

Why Choose HUPP?

HUPP not only eases your financial investment but also lets you focus on what truly matters – your education and your future. Enjoy top-notch instruction, personalized mentoring, industry internships, job shadowing opportunities, and an award-winning academic environment that fosters success.

Frequently Asked Questions

What documentation do I need to apply?

You will need to complete the FAFSA form, provide your high school records, and submit a deposit as well as any supporting documents as required by the admissions team.

Can HUPP be renewed?

Yes. Renewal is available for full-time students over eight semesters, as long as academic and program requirements are met.

Who can I contact for more information?

You can reach out to our admissions office via our Contact Us page for any queries.
