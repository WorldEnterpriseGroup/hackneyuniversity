Our Visionary Leaders

Hackney University is led by a dynamic team of forward-thinking educators and industry disruptors who embody our FlipSchool philosophy. With decades of combined experience in STEM, entrepreneurship, and global business, our leadership inspires innovation, nurtures talent, and champions a collaborative, multicultural environment.

In-Depth Leadership Bios

Learn more about the experiences and vision that drive our leadership team.

Dr. Alice Monroe – Chancellor & Visionary Founder

Dr. Alice Monroe established Hackney University with the core belief that education must evolve. Her career spans top academic institutions, groundbreaking research in alternative learning methods, and the creation of our innovative FlipSchool model. Under her leadership, Hackney University has grown into a beacon of progressive, student-led learning. Her accolades include international awards for educational innovation and influential publications in STEM education.

Mr. Brian Lee – Vice Chancellor & Strategic Business Leader

With a distinguished career in global business and technology, Mr. Brian Lee brings strategic vision and operational excellence to Hackney University. His extensive experience in launching successful tech startups and forging strong industry partnerships ensures our curriculum remains relevant and forward-thinking. Brian is dedicated to preparing students for the competitive job market of tomorrow.

Ms. Catherine Perez – Chief Innovation Officer

A pioneer in software innovation and product development, Ms. Catherine Perez has been instrumental in launching multiple award-winning SaaS products. As a strategic partner with global tech giants, she channels her expertise into mentoring our students and steering research projects that break new ground in computer science and digital entrepreneurship.

Our Leadership Vision & Core Values

At Hackney University, our leadership is united by a passion for transformative education. We champion the FlipSchool model, where every student is encouraged to lead while experienced mentors provide guidance. Our core values include innovation, collaboration, diversity, sustainability, and real-world impact.

Value What It Means to Us
Innovation Constantly challenging the traditional norms of education.
Collaboration Fostering a close-knit community where ideas flourish through shared leadership.
Diversity Embracing global perspectives to enrich our academic environment.
Sustainability Advancing green practices and sustainable methods in all our initiatives.
Real-World Impact Ensuring every student graduates with practical skills and valuable industry connections.
