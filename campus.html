Campus Overview

Experience an inspiring blend of tradition, innovation, and sophistication in the heart of Ravenswood, WV.

Our Campus Location

Situated at 313 Walnut St, our picturesque campus is nestled in the heart of Ravenswood. Within steps of downtown and breathtaking views of the Ohio River, Hackney University offers a blend of historic charm and modern sophistication.

Campus & Community Highlights

Discover the exceptional features that make our campus unique – from state-of-the-art facilities to luxury living spaces.

Research Residences

Our on-campus residences include dedicated research apartments designed for scholars who require a quiet, productive environment.

Co-Working Stations

Collaborative workspaces throughout campus foster innovation and interdisciplinary research, connecting students and faculty in dynamic environments.

Luxury Living & Family-Oriented Environment

Experience refined living with spacious, luxury accommodations and family-friendly facilities, ideal for students and their loved ones.

Location & Transportation

Perfectly positioned on Walnut St, our campus is just steps from downtown Ravenswood and the scenic Ohio River. Enjoy convenient bus access to Charleston, Parkersburg, and even direct services to our Mountain Research Lab in Grantsville, WV.

Frequently Asked Questions

What amenities are available on campus?

Campus amenities include research residences, co‐working stations, luxury accommodations, dining facilities, and recreational spaces.

How do I access transportation services?

Our campus is well connected through bus routes that serve Charleston, Parkersburg, and Grantsville. Check our website for detailed schedules.

Are there options for family housing?

Yes, Hackney University offers a variety of housing solutions designed to accommodate students with families, including family apartments and community resources.
