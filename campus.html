Our Campus Location

Located at 313 Walnut St in Ravenswood, WV, Hackney University boasts a unique campus spread along this historic street. With six outstanding buildings that serve as hubs for academic, administrative, and research excellence, our location sets the stage for innovative learning and community engagement.

Nestled along Walnut St, our campus enjoys easy access to the vibrant heart of Ravenswood. Nearby, the majestic Ohio River provides breathtaking views and a natural setting that complements our state-of-the-art facilities, while the dynamic local industry creates opportunities for student internships, research collaborations, and career networking.

Campus & Community Details

Our six-campus buildings stretch along Walnut St, forming an integrated environment where education meets innovation. The strategic location at 313 Walnut St not only provides a picturesque setting but also connects you seamlessly with the rest of Ravenswood. This vibrant town, home to roughly 5,500 residents, is enriched by its industrial roots and dynamic local businesses. The proximity to industry means that our students enjoy unique hands-on opportunities that bridge academic theory with real-world applications.

Whether you are strolling along Walnut St to catch a glimpse of our modern facilities or taking in the scenic views of the Ohio River, you will discover a campus that blends historical charm with contemporary academic innovation. Hackney University’s location fosters strong community ties, collaboration with local industries, and an environment where every student can thrive.
