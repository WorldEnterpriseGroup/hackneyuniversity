With these premium services, our rates reflect the exceptional value and personalized attention our students receive. Tuition rates are carefully structured to support Pell Grant eligibility while remaining competitive in the private university market. Hackney University Pell Promise (HUPP)

In our ongoing commitment to making excellence accessible, Hackney University proudly introduces the Hackney University Pell Promise (HUPP). Designed for eligible first-time, degree-seeking students who qualify for the Federal Pell Grant, HUPP covers the remaining tuition and mandatory fees after all scholarships and grants have been applied. This promise is renewable for up to eight semesters for qualifying undergraduate students.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Submit a Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) by the priority deadline.
  • Maintain a minimum High School GPA of 2.95 or better.
  • Confirm your intent to enroll by submitting your deposit in a timely manner.
  • Enroll as a traditional, full-time, first-year student and reside in our on-campus luxury housing.
  • Pursue your first bachelor’s degree.
  • Household Adjusted Gross Income (AGI) of $75,000 or less may qualify, subject to FAFSA determinations.

For more details on HUPP, please visit our Financial Aid page.

Additional Fee Note: International students are required to cover a mandatory health insurance fee. Please refer to our Finance page for details regarding domestic and international student health insurance requirements.
