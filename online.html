Revolutionize Your Learning Experience

At Hackney University, we have been paving the way in online education for over 20 years. Our dynamic online degree programs are designed to empower you with the latest digital tools and a comprehensive learning ecosystem. As a Microsoft Education partner, we integrate industry's best platforms & tools such as MS Teams, SharePoint, and a wide variety of online services to deliver engaging lectures and interactive sessions.

Every student is required to install our exclusive Focus Suite along with Focus Apps on a Windows 11 device, ensuring that you have a dedicated and secure digital environment for your studies. We pre-populate your computer with all essential educational software, provide robust security oversight, and employ proprietary Monitoring & Evaluation techniques to track your progress and effort meticulously.

Our online sessions are not only cutting-edge but also globally accessible (translated into over 80 languages for enhanced learning). Whether you're joining us fully online or opting for our hybrid programs that blend in-person campus experiences with digital learning, Hackney University offers a flexible, innovative, and supportive environment to transform your educational journey.

Join us today and take the first step towards a future filled with endless opportunities!

Experience the Digital Ecosystem

Our state-of-the-art digital platform leverages MS Teams and SharePoint to create virtual classrooms that mirror the vibrant atmosphere of our campus. Our Digital Platform

Powered by Microsoft's ecosystem, our digital learning platform offers a seamless interface for live classes, discussion forums, and collaborative group projects. Enhanced by MS Teams and SharePoint, it places state-of-the-art technology right at your fingertips.

Technical Requirements & Tools

To ensure maximum performance and security, every student must use a device running Windows 11. Our pre-configured Focus Suite, including Focus Apps, is installed on your computer to provide uninterrupted access to educational resources and monitoring tools.

Hybrid Programs - Best of Both Worlds

In addition to our comprehensive online degrees, Hackney University offers hybrid programs that blend on-campus engagement with digital flexibility. Attend select in-person sessions, campus events, and networking experiences while enjoying the convenience of online learning.

Global Reach & Multilingual Access

Our online sessions are translated into over 80 languages, ensuring that students from all corners of the globe can learn in their preferred language. This global inclusiveness has been one of our most celebrated features.

Dedicated Student Support

From technical assistance to personalized academic mentoring, our support network is designed to ensure your success. Access our Focus Guides (AI Mentors) in audio, text, or visual formats and join live help sessions to resolve any queries.
