A vibrant, multicultural environment nurturing STEM excellence and family values.

Our Unique Approach

Discover a transformative education in our small, close-knit, and culturally diverse community dedicated to experiential learning.

FlipSchool Model

Lead your own learning journey in classes where students drive collaboration and professors provide mentorship – no traditional lectures, only dynamic interactive sessions.

Alternative Learning

Immerse yourself in outdoor meetups, park discussions, library sessions, and creative spaces like our CoCreation Café, where ideas blossom.

Experiential Internships

Every student participates in a hands-on internship—bridging academic theories with cutting-edge industry experience and real-world challenges.

Global Diversity

Join a vibrant, multicultural community of fewer than 50 students, where perspectives from around the world enrich every discussion.

Family Focus

Our campus values both academic and family life. We host community events and offer support to help you balance studies with personal growth.

STEM & Sustainability

With a strong focus on STEM, innovation, and sustainable practices, our programs are designed to empower future leaders in technology and green initiatives.

A Legacy of Collaborative Innovation

Founded on the idea that learning should be a shared journey, Hackney University has redefined education with our groundbreaking FlipSchool model. Our small community of under 50 students means that every individual is known, and every experience is personal. We believe that real growth comes from exploring new ideas outside the traditional classroom—whether during a quiet walk in a park, an intensive group discussion at a local café, or a hands-on project in our campus garden.

Our curriculum challenges the status quo. There are no lecture halls here—only spaces for exploration, collaboration, and creative thinking. Faculty serve as mentors who provide guidance and answer your most pressing questions, ensuring that you have both the freedom to innovate and the support to succeed.

To empower a globally diverse community through innovative, student-led learning. At Hackney University, we nurture talent with alternative educational experiences, mandatory internships, and an unwavering focus on STEM and sustainable practices.
