Empower Your Future Without a Degree

At Hackney University, we believe that learning is a lifelong journey. Our non-degree programs are crafted for auditors, professionals, and anyone with a passion for knowledge. Whether you're looking to upgrade your skills or explore new fields, our dynamic courses will inspire and empower you.

Begin your journey today and discover the benefits of flexible, innovative, and accessible education designed just for you.

Eligibility Requirements

General Requirements

Prospective non-degree students should be at least 18 years of age and possess a high school diploma or equivalent, although proven life experience may also be considered.

Financial Aid Restrictions

Please note that non-degree students are ineligible for federal financial aid, though limited scholarships might be available.

Course Capacity

Enrollment is subject to course capacity. Courses fill up quickly, so early registration is encouraged.

Application Process

Step 1: Submit Your Online Application

Complete our easy-to-use online application form to get started.

Step 2: Pay Application Fee

Securely pay the application fee online. (Fee details vary based on the selected course.)

Step 3: Submit Required Documents

Upload your transcripts, identification and any other supporting documents.

Step 4: Receive Your Confirmation

Once your application is processed, you will receive your course registration confirmation by email.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I take online courses?

Yes, our non-degree courses are offered online and in a hybrid format to suit your schedule.

Are credits transferable to a degree program?

While our non-degree courses are not directly applicable toward a degree, some credits may be transferable if you later enroll in a degree program.

How many courses can I enroll in?

Non-degree students may enroll in up to 5 courses per term to help maintain a balanced workload.
