Overview

At Hackney University, we understand that investing in your future is more than a financial decision – it’s a commitment to excellence. Our Financial Aid Office is dedicated to bridging the gap between your dreams and the cost of a premium, boutique education. Whether you are applying for grants, scholarships, or federal aid, our team is here to support you every step of the way.

Types of Financial Assistance

  • Grants & Scholarships: Awarded based on merit and need, these funds do not require repayment. Explore options designed to lighten your financial load.
  • Federal Aid: Eligible students can secure support through FAFSA. Our exceptional advisors guide you through each step to optimize available funding.
  • HUPP: The Hackney University Pell Promise (HUPP) drastically reduces your tuition for households earning $75K or less. Visit our HUPP page for more details.
  • Work-Study & Employment Opportunities: Through internships, job shadowing, and specialized work-study programs, you can offset education expenses while gaining valuable industry experience.

Application Process

Our streamlined application process is designed for simplicity and effectiveness:

  1. Complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) by the priority deadline.
  2. Submit the Hackney University Financial Aid Application online.
  3. Provide necessary documentation including transcripts, personal statements, and proof of household income.
  4. Consult with our Financial Aid Advisors to customize your aid package.
  5. Review your personalized financial aid notification and award package.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for financial aid?

Simply complete your FAFSA, then fill in the Hackney University Financial Aid Application. Our advisors will be in touch to assist you throughout the process.

What documents do I need to provide?

Prepare your transcripts, proof of household income, personal statement, and any additional supporting documentation as required.

Who can I speak to for assistance?

Our Financial Aid Office is available to answer all your queries. Contact us via email or phone, or visit our Contact Page.
