Our Exceptional Team

At Hackney University, our success is driven by a passionate group of faculty and staff who lead by example. Our faculty are renowned for their industry expertise—producing innovative SaaS products, securing global partnerships, and mentoring the next generation of leaders in STEM and business. Our supportive staff provide an unwavering backbone to our small, diverse community, ensuring every student receives personalized attention and guidance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about how our faculty and staff support your educational journey.

How do faculty mentor students in the FlipSchool model?

Our faculty empower students to lead projects and discussions while providing essential guidance, feedback, and real-world insights. They serve as collaborative mentors rather than traditional lecturers.

What support do staff provide to ensure student success?

Our dedicated staff manage student services, IT infrastructures, and community engagement initiatives to create a seamless, supportive environment where innovation and collaboration can thrive.

How are industry connections integrated into the curriculum?

Our faculty and staff maintain close partnerships with leading technology companies and global businesses. They actively facilitate internships and real-world projects that ensure our students gain invaluable industry experience.
