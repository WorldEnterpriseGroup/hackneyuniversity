Welcome, Future Visitors & Families!

At Hackney University, we understand that a campus visit is not just a tour – it is an immersive experience. Our visit days are designed to offer you a full picture of campus life, from interactive sessions with faculty to hands-on demonstrations in our cutting-edge labs, and even leisurely strolls through our historic quads.

Whether you're exploring your future academic home or seeking inspiration for a family day out, our friendly staff and detailed tour itineraries ensure you get the most out of your visit.

Enjoy exclusive campus insights, relaxed meet-and-greets with current students, and indulge in the senses with beautifully landscaped grounds and inspiring architecture.

Popular Visit Periods

Spring Open House

Each March, our Spring Open House invites prospective students and families to explore our campus through interactive sessions, mini-lectures, and guided tours amid blooming landscapes.

Summer Experience Days

In June and July, enjoy outdoor events, sports demonstrations, and casual Q&A sessions that capture the lively essence of our campus during the golden summer months.

Fall Preview & Orientation

September brings the buzz of new beginnings. Our Fall Preview Days combine academic presentations with campus tours to provide clarity as you step into the upcoming academic year.

Travel & Local Area Tips

Need help getting here? Hackney University is conveniently located near major highways and public transit lines.

  • Rental car services and taxi stands available at the main entrance.
  • Bus routes connect from downtown hubs to our campus.
  • Local accommodations offer special rates for visiting families.
  • Explore energy efficient shuttle services on-campus.

Parking & Public Transit

Our campus provides ample parking facilities along with dedicated drop-off zones and public transit stops:

  1. Visitor Parking: Located in the North Lot with complimentary 3-hour parking.
  2. Campus Shuttles: Available to transport visitors from designated parking areas.
  3. Nearby Transit: Connect easily via regional bus services; check our contact page for schedules.

Frequently Asked Questions

What parking facilities are available on campus?

Our campus offers multiple visitor parking options with ample space. Complimentary 3-hour parking is available in designated lots and campus shuttles are provided to transport you from the parking area to your destination.

How early should I arrive for my scheduled visit?

We recommend arriving at least 15 minutes before your scheduled tour to allow time for parking and check-in.

Are group tours available for families and organizations?

Yes, we offer group tours for families, school groups, and organizations. Please contact our admissions office to arrange a personalized group tour.
