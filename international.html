Welcome, International Students!

At Hackney University, we take pride in welcoming students from every corner of the world. Our commitment to diversity, global excellence, and personalized support ensures your transition into university life is both smooth and rewarding.

Why Choose Hackney University?

  • World-renowned education and research opportunities
  • Culturally diverse and inclusive campus community
  • Dedicated international student support & counseling
  • Generous scholarship and financial aid options
  • Comprehensive career and internship programs

Application Process

Follow these simple steps to join our vibrant international community:

  1. Submit your online application through our Application Portal.
  2. Upload your required documents and academic transcripts.
  3. Wait for an invitation to an interview or admission test.
  4. Receive your acceptance letter and next steps from our international office.

Visa and Immigration Support

Visa Requirements & Application Tips

Our international office provides detailed guidance on securing your student visa including document checklists, interview preparation, and application procedures. Visit the UK Government website for more details.

Additional Immigration Support

Our advisors are on hand to help you understand all the necessary steps for a successful visa application process. Explore our support resources for further assistance.

Support Services

  • Language Support
  • Housing Assistance
  • Counseling & Wellness

Our language support programs ensure that every international student can adjust academically and socially. Conversations, writing workshops and coaching sessions are available.

We assist in finding suitable housing options that meet your needs, whether on-campus or off-campus, and guide you through the leasing process.

Our counseling and wellness center offers professional support to help you adjust and thrive in a new cultural environment.
