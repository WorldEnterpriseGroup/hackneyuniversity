Program Overview

An advanced curriculum blending deep theoretical knowledge with cutting-edge practical applications.

The Master’s of Computer Science program at Hackney University is tailored for ambitious professionals and graduates ready to engage in sophisticated research and advanced technical coursework. Our program is research intensive, designed to foster innovative thinking and equip you with the skills to tackle complex problems in emerging areas of technology.

Students will have the opportunity to work alongside global experts in state-of-the-art research labs, participate in pioneering projects, and benefit from an industry-aligned curriculum that emphasizes both academic rigour and real-world applications.

Explore advanced modules, specialized electives, and a robust thesis or capstone component that prepares you for leadership roles or doctoral studies. Join us in shaping the future of technology.

Innovative Research

Engage with advanced research projects in areas such as artificial intelligence, big data, and cybersecurity using our state-of-the-art facilities.

Collaborative Learning

Benefit from close collaboration with faculty experts, participate in dynamic seminars, and attend international conferences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to help you understand our program and the application process.

What are the entry requirements?

Applicants should have a solid background in computer science or a related field, with a minimum undergraduate GPA as specified by the admissions office.

Can I choose a research topic?

Yes, students work closely with faculty advisors to develop their own research proposals and select topics aligned with their interests and industry trends.

Is there a thesis requirement?

The program offers both thesis and non-thesis options, allowing you to choose the path that best suits your career plans.

What career prospects are available?

Graduates can pursue careers in advanced research, academia, industry innovation, and leadership roles in top tech companies.

Are there scholarship options?

Yes, numerous scholarships and assistantships are available. Please contact our financial aid office for detailed eligibility information.

Is part-time study available?

In select cases, part-time study options are offered to accommodate working professionals. Speak with our admissions team for more details.
