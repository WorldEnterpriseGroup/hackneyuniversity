Course Overview

Gain advanced expertise in human resources management and research.

The Master's of Human Resources program at Hackney University is meticulously crafted for professionals seeking to elevate their strategic, analytical, and leadership skills in the dynamic world of HR. This program blends cutting-edge research with practical applications, enabling you to drive innovation and transformational change within organizations.

Through a combination of evening lectures, interactive case studies, and real-world projects, you will explore topics such as global workforce management, HR analytics, and digital transformation, preparing you to meet the complex demands of a competitive global market.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our Master's program in Human Resources.

What are the admission requirements?

Candidates should possess an undergraduate degree with a solid academic record and relevant experience in HR or related fields.

How is the program structured?

The schedule combines evening lectures, interactive seminars, and weekend workshops for maximum flexibility.

Full-Time or Part-Time?

We offer both full-time and part-time study options tailored for working professionals.

Are there internship opportunities?

Yes, our curriculum integrates collaborative projects with industry partners to offer practical experience.

What career paths can I pursue?

Graduates are well-prepared for senior roles in HR management, consulting, analytics, and talent strategy.

How do I apply?

You can apply online, by phone, or via postal mail. Our admissions team is here to guide you every step of the way.
