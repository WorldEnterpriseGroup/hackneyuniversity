Program Overview

Pushing the boundaries of service excellence and innovation.

The Doctor of Philosophy in Hospitality and Tourism Management at Hackney University is designed for visionary scholars and industry pioneers. This rigorous program empowers you to conduct groundbreaking research on global hospitality trends and tourism management practices with an unwavering commitment to excellence and innovation.

Students engage in comprehensive coursework, collaborative research projects, and interdisciplinary seminars. By challenging recognized norms and embracing new methodologies, our PhD candidates develop robust academic theses that shape the future of travel, leisure, and service excellence.

Pioneering Research

Explore international hospitality trends and contribute original insights that redefine industry standards.

Innovative Strategies

Develop methodologies and operational models that challenge conventional practices and stimulate transformative change.

Program Timeline

An outline of the research and study progression over the duration of your PhD.

The Doctoral program spans over 4 to 5 years, combining coursework, comprehensive examinations, field research, and a dissertation defense.

Year Focus Areas
Year 1 Advanced Coursework & Research Methods
Year 2 Field Research & Data Collection
Year 3 Analysis, Dissertation Proposal & Comprehensive Exams
Year 4 Dissertation Writing & Peer Review
Year 5 Dissertation Defense & Publication

Frequently Asked Questions

Your queries about the PhD program answered by our admissions team.

What are the admission requirements?

Applicants must hold a relevant master’s degree, demonstrate research potential, and provide a detailed research proposal.

What research support is offered?

Our program offers access to state-of-the-art research facilities, funding opportunities, and international collaboration networks.

Is teaching experience required?

While not mandatory, prior teaching or industry experience is highly valued and may enhance your research perspective.
