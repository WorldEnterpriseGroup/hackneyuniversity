Course Overview

Our DBA program is designed for senior professionals and scholars who aspire to transform business practice through breakthrough research.

The Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) at Hackney University offers a unique blend of academic rigor and practical insight. Tailored for those who have already excelled in leadership roles, this program challenges you to rethink established theories, engage in pioneering research, and effect meaningful change in the global business arena.

Enrol in an intensive program combining expert-led seminars, collaborative workshops, and one-on-one mentorship while you work on a dissertation that matters. Experience a curriculum built to foster innovative strategies and drive transformative leadership.

Innovative Research

Engage with breakthrough methodologies and leverage advanced analytical tools to pioneer new business solutions.

Strategic Leadership

Develop transformative strategies that address complex global challenges and steer dynamic organizations.

Core Modules

Dive deep into advanced topics designed to revolutionize your understanding of global business dynamics.

Our curriculum is centered on immersive research, strategic analysis, and ethical governance. Each module is structured to challenge conventional paradigms and empower you with the tools for transformative change.

  • Advanced Research Methods
  • Disruptive Innovation in Global Markets
  • Strategic Financial Decision Making
  • Quantitative Analysis for Business
  • Thesis Design and Execution
  • Organizational Transformation
  • Ethical Governance & Global Impact
  • Emerging Technologies in Business
