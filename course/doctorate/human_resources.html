Program Overview

An intensive, research-driven journey toward academic and professional excellence.

The Doctor of Philosophy in Human Resource Management at Hackney University is designed for dedicated professionals and scholars seeking to pioneer innovative research in the field of HR. This program emphasizes original inquiry, rigorous data analysis, and critical theoretical development, preparing graduates for academic careers as well as leadership roles in industry and consultancy.

Throughout your doctoral studies, you will engage in advanced seminars, collaborate with renowned faculty mentors, and produce original research that contributes to the evolution of human capital strategies worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries regarding our Doctoral program in HR Management.

What are the admission criteria?

Applicants must hold a relevant master’s degree with a strong academic record and demonstrate significant research potential.

Is the program full-time?

Our program is designed for both full-time and part-time study, accommodating professionals in the field.

What research support is offered?

Candidates benefit from extensive mentorship, state-of-the-art research facilities, and opportunities to publish their work.

How long does the program last?

The doctoral journey typically spans 3-5 years, depending on individual research progress and chosen study mode.

Are there teaching opportunities?

Yes – many of our PhD candidates gain practical teaching experience as part of their academic development.

How do I apply?

You can apply online, by phone, or download the application package and mail it. Our team is here to help you through the process.
