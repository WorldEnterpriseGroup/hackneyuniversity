Bachelor's Degree of Allied Health

An interdisciplinary program blending holistic health practices with both Eastern and Western health philosophies.

  • Home

Program Overview

A comprehensive journey into holistic and biomedical health

The Bachelor's Degree of Allied Health is an interdisciplinary program designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of holistic health practices. By integrating Eastern philosophies such as Ayurveda, yoga, and meditation with the strong foundations of Western medicine, this program emphasizes the interconnected nature of the body and mind.

In addition to core biomedical subjects, students will explore cutting-edge research in areas including the gut biome, fascia, and interstitium, alongside mental wellness. Our collaboration with Allied Care Inc. and their staffing division, Allied Health Group, ensures access to valuable internship opportunities and career pathways that allow you to apply your knowledge in real-world healthcare settings.

Core Courses and Focus Areas

Diverse modules bridging traditional and modern health systems

Foundational Health Sciences

  • Anatomy and Physiology
  • The Gut Biome and Digestive Health
  • The Interstitium and Fascia: The Body's Hidden Systems
  • The Fire Within: Understanding Metabolism, Nutrition, and Exercise

Eastern Health Philosophies

  • Ayurveda: Principles of Doshas, Nutrition, and Daily Routines
  • Yoga: The Mind-Body Connection
  • Meditation and Breathwork for Mental Wellness
  • Japanese Shinrin-yoku (Forest Bathing): Nature's Role in Healing

Western Health Systems

  • Germ Theory vs. Terrain Theory: Complementary Approaches to Health
  • History and Financial Structures of Western Medicine
  • Evidence-Based Medicine and Research Fundamentals

Global Health and Cultural Practices

  • Comparative Health Modalities Around the World
  • Integrative Health Practices in Modern Healthcare Settings
  • Sleep Science: The Foundation of Mental and Physical Wellness

Practical and Professional Development

  • Internship with Allied Health Group for Hands-On Experience
  • Ethics and Cultural Sensitivity in Allied Health
  • Career Development: Building a Future in Allied Health Professions

Learning Outcomes

Graduate ready to transform healthcare

  • Apply both Western biomedical approaches and Eastern holistic philosophies to promote health and wellness.
  • Assess and understand the interconnected systems of the body and mind, including the fascia, gut biome, and interstitium.
  • Gain practical experience through internships with Allied Care Inc. and Allied Health Group.
  • Work confidently in diverse healthcare settings, from traditional clinics to holistic wellness centers.

The Allied Health Philosophy

A holistic approach to wellness

At Hackney University, we believe in the fundamental interconnectedness of body and mind. Our Allied Health program is designed not only to impart scientific and clinical knowledge but also to foster a deep appreciation for holistic healing methods. By blending Eastern wisdom with Western science, our graduates are uniquely prepared to drive innovation and promote wellness on a global scale.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your queries about the Allied Health program answered.

What are the admission requirements?

Applicants should have a high school diploma or equivalent with a keen interest in health sciences and wellness practices.

What career paths can I pursue?

Graduates can work in hospitals, clinics, holistic wellness centers, and various allied health roles both domestically and internationally.

How do I apply?

You can apply online, call our admissions team, or download an application form available on our website.
