Course Overview

Where tradition meets innovation in education.

The Bachelor’s of Education program at Hackney University seamlessly integrates traditional pedagogical practices with cutting-edge teaching methodologies. Emphasizing the critical importance of campus life, family balance, and community engagement, our course empowers future educators to develop dynamic and student-focused learning environments.

Our curriculum covers everything from conventional classroom management to alternative learning strategies such as flipped classrooms and homeschooling statistics. We’re also pioneers in integrating AI in education—teaching you to develop AI Tudors and create personalized AI Teachers that serve as private tutors for each student.

SMILE Method

Embrace Self-Motivated Impact Learning (SMILE) with Montessori-inspired techniques and create environments that shape students rather than traditional lectures.

AI-Enhanced Learning

Build robust AI tutor models that allow every student a personalized interaction, making education adaptive and future-ready.

Core Modules

Essential studies designed to redefine modern education.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our Bachelor’s of Education program.

What are the admission requirements?

Candidates should demonstrate a strong academic background with a passion for teaching, alongside evidence of community involvement.

What is the focus of the program?

We blend traditional pedagogical principles with innovative teaching strategies, including AI integration and the SMILE Method, to foster a holistic learning environment.

Is there practical training?

Absolutely. Our program incorporates internships, classroom observations, and hands-on projects with local schools and alternative learning centers.

Do I get a personalized AI tutor?

Yes, our curriculum includes training on developing AI tutors, ensuring every student benefits from a personalized learning companion.

What career paths are available?

Graduates become innovative educators, curriculum designers, educational consultants and leaders in alternative learning environments.

How do I apply?

You can apply online, contact us by phone, or download our application form. Our admissions office is ready to help.
