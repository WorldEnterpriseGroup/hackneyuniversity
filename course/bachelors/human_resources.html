Course Overview

Discover the science and art of managing human capital.

The Bachelor’s of Human Resources program at Hackney University is designed to equip you with the advanced skills necessary for managing and developing an organization's most valuable asset—its people. With an emphasis on modern HR theory, ethical practices, and strategic leadership, you'll learn to navigate complex labor challenges and drive organizational success.

Mixing rigorous academic coursework with real-world case studies, interactive seminars, and hands-on projects, our program prepares you for diverse roles in talent acquisition, employee engagement, and organizational development.

Talent & Development

Master strategies for recruiting, developing, and retaining top talent in dynamic work environments.

Employee Engagement

Learn to build a vibrant organizational culture that maximizes employee satisfaction and performance.

Core Modules

Essential courses designed to provide a comprehensive foundation in HR management.

Our curriculum emphasizes both theory and practice, covering all facets of human resources management.

  • Principles of Human Resource Management
  • Organizational Behavior
  • Employment Law
  • Workforce Planning & Analytics
  • Compensation & Benefits
  • Talent Acquisition & Retention
  • Training & Development
  • Strategic HR Management

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our Bachelor’s of Human Resources program.

What are the admission requirements?

Candidates should have a strong academic record, particularly in subjects such as English, Mathematics, and Social Studies.

What is the focus of the program?

The program covers strategic HR management, employee engagement, compensation & benefits, and organizational development.

Are there internship opportunities?

Yes, our curriculum integrates internships and hands-on projects with leading organizations.

What career paths are available?

Graduates can pursue careers in HR management, recruitment, employee relations, training & development, and more.

Do you offer study abroad?

Yes, exchange programs with international partner institutions allow you to gain a global perspective on HR.

How do I apply?

You can apply online, by phone, or download our application form and mail it. Our admissions office is here to assist.
