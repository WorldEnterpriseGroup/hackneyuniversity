Course Overview

Blending regional charm with international expertise.

The Bachelor of Hospitality & Tourism Management program at Hackney University is meticulously designed for future leaders in the hospitality industry. Located in the heart of West Virginia, our program leverages the rich regional tourism offerings – from scenic hiking trails to waterfalls – while also drawing on global best practices from centers in Bali, Costa Rica, and California.

Our strategic partnerships with boutique bed and breakfasts, aparthotels, and leading tourism companies, along with collaborations with ballroom dance coaches, interior design professionals, and yoga retreat experts, provide you with hands-on experience in a diverse array of hospitality services. Whether it’s managing cafes, flipping rooms, acquiring property, or marketing furnished spaces, our curriculum ensures you graduate with a plethora of skills requisite for success in both hospitality and tourism management.

Local & Global Partnerships

Learn from industry leaders and immerse yourself in hands-on experiences across West Virginia and worldwide destinations.

Diverse Skill Acquisition

Gain skills in cafe management, event planning, property acquisition, interior design, and marketing to excel in tourism ventures.

Comprehensive training across multiple facets of hospitality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your queries about our program answered by our expert team.

What are the admission requirements?

Applicants should demonstrate academic excellence and a passion for hospitality, with previous experience or internships preferred.

Do you offer internships?

Yes, we have partnerships with local and international hospitality businesses offering extensive internship opportunities.

Will I gain practical experience?

Absolutely. Our curriculum includes hands-on training through live projects, workshops, and real industry collaborations.

What career paths are available?

Graduates pursue careers in hotel management, tourism marketing, property management, event planning, and more.

Is international exposure provided?

Yes, our partnerships and study tours in Bali, Costa Rica, and California offer global exposure.

How do I apply?

You can apply online, call our admissions office, or download the application form. Our team is here to help.
