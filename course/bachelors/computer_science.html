Course Overview

An intensive program that builds a deep foundation in both theory and practical applications.

The Bachelor's of Computer Science program at Hackney University is designed to empower students with a comprehensive understanding of algorithms, data structures, programming languages, and systems design. Through a blend of lectures, hands-on laboratories, and innovative projects, you will develop the skills necessary to tackle the challenges of today’s rapidly evolving tech industry.

Engage in collaborative projects, cutting-edge research, and vital internships that give you a competitive edge. Our curriculum remains dynamic and current, ensuring graduates are well prepared for advanced studies or immediate entry into the professional world.

Discover exciting topics ranging from artificial intelligence and cybersecurity to mobile computing and software engineering. Take the first step toward a future in technology at Hackney University.

Industry Relevance

Our program is designed in consultation with industry experts so you gain the skills that modern tech companies demand—whether it be software development, cybersecurity, or data analytics.

Innovative Research

Access advanced labs and research centers to work on projects that push the boundaries of current technology and foster creative problem solving.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your queries answered—learn more about our program and enrollment process.

What are the entry requirements?

Applicants should have a strong academic background in mathematics and science. While prior programming experience is beneficial, it is not compulsory.

Do I need prior programming experience?

No prior programming experience is required. Our introductory modules will equip you with the basics before advancing to higher-level concepts.

Is internship integrated into the curriculum?

Yes. Our program includes internship opportunities and hands-on projects that allow you to gain real-world experience while studying.

What career opportunities are available?

Graduates can explore careers in software development, data analysis, cybersecurity, research, and many other tech-related fields.

Are part-time options available?

We offer full-time programs and, in some cases, part-time study options. Contact our admissions office for more details regarding your circumstances.

Do you offer study abroad programs?

Yes, there are exchange programs with partner institutions around the world. Learn more during our admissions consultation.
