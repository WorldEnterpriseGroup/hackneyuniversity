Welcome to Life at HU

At Hackney University, we are a small community of fewer than 50 students from around the globe. Our tight-knit environment means that every voice is heard—and every journey is shared. Embracing our unique FlipSchool model, our students drive initiatives while our professors are there for clarifications and guidance. Forget dull lecture halls; we learn through shared trips, interactive meetups, and inspiring outdoor sessions.

  • Personalized, collaborative learning
  • Shared trips, hikes & creative meetups
  • Active internships and corporate engagement
  • Alternative, experiential learning environments
  • A global community that values creativity and service

Campus in Action

Our revolutionary learning philosophy means that students lead the charge—driving projects, organizing study groups, and creating their own learning modules. Professors are never lecturing from the front; they provide guidance as mentors and answer questions when needed. Here, learning is an active, experiential journey.

This approach ensures that every class, every project, and every collaborative session is driven by passion and creativity.

Shared Journeys & Outdoor Learning

We believe that true learning happens outside the traditional classroom. Whether it's a quiet walk in the park, a reflective hike with a professor, or an engaging discussion during a library meetup, our shared trips help ideas settle and foster deeper connections.

The CoCreation Cafe is the pulsating heart of our campus—a welcoming space for quiet study, inspiration, and collaboration. It's where students who appreciate a peaceful ambiance come together to focus, brainstorm, and enjoy the benefits of shared creativity.

Alternative Learning & Global Engagement

Our teaching philosophy is as diverse as our student body. Coming from all over the world, our students embrace alternative learning opportunities—volunteering with non-profits, nurturing campus gardens, and participating in sustainable landscaping projects. These experiences enrich our academic journey and foster a proactive approach to learning.

  • Volunteering in local non-profits
  • Hands-on involvement in campus gardening
  • Collaborations that inspire sustainable living
  • Global perspectives and cultural exchange

Corporate Engagement & Internships

At Hackney University, internships are a fundamental part of our FlipSchool approach. Corporations frequently visit our campus to engage with our tight-knit community, offering real-world challenges that enrich our hands-on learning. Every student is required to complete an internship—ensuring that the transition from academia to industry is natural and seamless.

Campus Life FAQs

What is the FlipSchool teaching model?

Instead of traditional lectures, our FlipSchool model empowers students to lead initiatives while professors provide guidance and answer questions. This dynamic model fosters creativity and personal growth.

How do shared trips and outdoor learning sessions work?

We organize regular outings—ranging from hikes and park meetups to visits at libraries and local eateries—designed to encourage reflection, creative discussion, and a deeper connection with nature.

What internship opportunities are available?

Every student is required to complete an internship as part of our educational journey. Our strong ties with corporate partners ensure that you gain practical experience and build professional networks.
